Project : Smart Crop Recommendation System Using 

Description:
    Farmers often struggle to choose the right crop due to varying soil conditions, water availability, local climate, and current market demand. Many digital tools are difficult for uneducated or semi-literate farmers because they require typing or technical knowledge.
Our solution is a simple n8n-based form system where farmers provide minimal inputs:
Name
Email 
Soil type (via checkboxes)
Water availability (Normal, High, Low, No water)
Location (typed by the farmer)

The system uses n8n workflow and AI to recommend the most suitable crops based on:

Soil type and water availability
Local climate/weather conditions
Current market demand/prices

Results are provided in both English and Kannada and sent directly to the farmer via email, helping them make informed crop choices.

Key Features:

Simple, user-friendly form with minimal input
Crop suggestions consider market trends for profitability
Recommendations in English and Kannada
Email notification for farmer record keeping
Optional weather integration for better accuracy
Works entirely in n8n, no frontend coding required

Impact:
Empowers farmers to choose crops that are suitable for their land, sustainable, and profitable, reducing crop loss, optimizing resources, and increasing income.
